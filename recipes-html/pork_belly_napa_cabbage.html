Tender Braised Pork Belly with Savory Napa Cabbage
Rich, melt-in-your-mouth pork belly slow-braised with Asian flavors

15 min
Cook Time
3 hrs

4

3
Remove the pork belly and set aside. Add the onion and garlic to the pot and cook for 2 minutes until softened.
4
Stir in chicken broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, and ginger. Return the pork belly to the pot, skin-side up.
5
Cover the pot and transfer it to the oven. Braise for 2.5 hours or until the pork is tender.
6
In the last 15 minutes of cooking, stir in the napa cabbage and cook until wilted.
7
Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
