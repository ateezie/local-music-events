2

3

ü•© Ground Pork Instructions

1
Meanwhile, peel and roughly chop 2 cloves of garlic. In a bowl, combine the pork and chopped garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Gently mix and form into 2 patties, about ¬Ω-inch thick.
2
In a medium nonstick pan (or large for 4 servings), heat a drizzle of olive oil on medium-high until hot.
3
Add the patties. Cook 5 to 6 minutes, or until browned on first side.
4
Flip the patties (carefully, as the oil may splatter) and evenly top with the fontina. Loosely cover the pan with foil.
5
Cook 5 to 6 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the patties are browned and cooked through.
6
Leaving any browned bits (or fond) in the pan, transfer to a work surface. Carefully drain off and discard any excess oil.
