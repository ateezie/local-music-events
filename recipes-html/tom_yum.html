Thai Coconut Shrimp Tom Yum
Spicy, creamy shrimp soup with coconut milk, lemongrass, and fresh herbs
Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
15 min
Serves
2-3
Difficulty
Easy

300g shrimp, peeled
2 cups coconut milk
2 cups chicken broth
1 stalk lemongrass
3 kaffir lime leaves
1 tbsp fish sauce
1 tsp chili paste
Juice of 1 lime
Fresh cilantro for garnish
Red chili slices for garnish

üç¥ Equipment

  • Large saucepan or pot
  • Knife for prep
  • Cutting board
  • Ladle
  • Serving bowls
  • Citrus juicer (optional)

üìã Instructions

1
Bruise the lemongrass stalk with the back of a knife and cut into 2-inch pieces. Tear the kaffir lime leaves slightly to release their oils.
2
In a large saucepan, gently simmer coconut milk with lemongrass, lime leaves, and chicken broth over medium heat for 5 minutes. Do not boil vigorously.
3
Add chili paste and fish sauce to the simmering broth. Stir well to combine and let flavors meld for 2-3 minutes.
4
Add shrimp to the pot and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and are just cooked through. Do not overcook.
5
Remove from heat and stir in fresh lime juice. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve immediately garnished with cilantro and red chili slices.
