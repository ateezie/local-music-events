Egg Roll in a Bowl
All the flavors of egg rolls without the wrapper
Prep Time
10 min
Cook Time
20 min
Serves
4-6
Difficulty
Easy

üõí Ingredients

1 lb ground beef (or pork, chicken, turkey)
1 bag (14-16 oz) coleslaw mix
1 medium onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
Red pepper flakes (optional)
Green onions for garnish
Sesame seeds for garnish

üç¥ Equipment

  • Large skillet or wok
  • Spatula or wooden spoon
  • Cutting board
  • Sharp knife
  • Measuring spoons
  • Serving bowls

üìã Instructions

1
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat, breaking it into chunks. Cook until no longer pink and slightly caramelized.
2
Add diced onion to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
3
Add minced garlic and ginger, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn.
4
Add coleslaw mix and cook for 3-5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp, maintaining some crunch.
5
Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1-2 minutes more. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
