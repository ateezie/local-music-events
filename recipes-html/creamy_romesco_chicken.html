ü•© Pork Chops

1
Pat pork dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
2
In medium nonstick pan (large for 4 servings), heat drizzle of olive oil on medium-high until hot.
3
Add seasoned pork. Cook 4-6 minutes per side for medium (center may be slightly pink), or until browned and cooked to desired doneness.
4
Transfer to cutting board; let rest at least 5 minutes. Slice crosswise and serve with garlic bread and roasted broccoli. Top with creamy romesco and garnish with remaining parmesan.

ü•© Flank Steaks

1
Pat steaks dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
2
In medium nonstick pan (large for 4 servings), heat drizzle of olive oil on medium-high until hot.
3
Add seasoned steaks. Cook 4-6 minutes per side for medium-rare (125¬∞F), or until browned and cooked to desired doneness.
4
Transfer to cutting board; let rest at least 5 minutes. Find direction of muscle grain and slice crosswise against the grain. Serve with garlic bread and roasted broccoli. Top with creamy romesco and garnish with remaining parmesan.

üêü Salmon Fillets

1
Pat fish dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper on both sides. Season only the skinless side with Italian seasoning.
2
In medium nonstick pan (large for 4 servings), heat drizzle of olive oil on medium-high until hot.
3
Add seasoned fish, skin side down. Cook 5-7 minutes until skin is browned and crispy.
4
Flip and cook 1-2 minutes until lightly browned and cooked through. Turn off heat. Serve with garlic bread and roasted broccoli. Top with creamy romesco and garnish with remaining parmesan.
