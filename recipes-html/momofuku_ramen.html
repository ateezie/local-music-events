David Chang's Classic Momofuku Ramen
The original from-scratch ramen with bacon-enriched pork broth
Prep Time
30 min
Cook Time
4-6 hrs
Serves
8-10
Difficulty

üõí Broth Ingredients

12 cups water

2 green onions, chopped
1 onion, quartered
1 large carrot, chopped

üßÇ Seasoning Mix

üçú Serving

üç¥ Equipment

  • Large stockpot (6+ quart capacity)

  • Cheesecloth
  • Baking sheet
  • Slotted spoon

üìã Instructions

1

2
Rinse shiitakes, add to pot. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer for 30 minutes. Remove shiitakes (save for pickling).
3
Add chicken to pot. Simmer gently 1 hour, skimming foam. Remove when meat pulls from bones easily. Shred meat, set aside.
4
Preheat oven to 400¬∞F. Roast pork bones 30 minutes until golden. Add to broth with bacon. Simmer 45 minutes, remove bacon.
5
Continue simmering bones 1 hour. Add vegetables, simmer 1 more hour. Strain through cheesecloth-lined strainer.
6
Season broth: whisk seasoning ingredients, add half to broth, taste and adjust. Should be "not quite too salty but almost."
7
To serve: heat broth, cook noodles, arrange in bowls with shredded chicken, ramen eggs, nori, and green onions. Ladle hot broth over.
