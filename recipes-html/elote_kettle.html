üí° Notes & Tips

Serving: Best served immediately while chips are still warm and crispy.

Spice Level: Adjust chili de arbol dust to taste - start with less and add more.

Make Ahead: Prep all toppings in advance, but assemble just before serving to prevent soggy chips.
